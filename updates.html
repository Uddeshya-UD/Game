New Legend

  1. Bolt action sniper rifle.
  2. Medium to long range.
  3. Does massive damage to shields while energized (consume a shield battery while equipped to energize.
  4. Unique VFX trails and SFX while energized.

Map update: World's Edge

  1. Multi-level Planet Harvester at the map's center
  2. Capitol City has been split into Fragment East and Fragment West
  3. Lava fissures and updrafts - you'll take damage for falling into the fissure then be carried back to map level on a updraft though other players can shoot at you as you float upward
